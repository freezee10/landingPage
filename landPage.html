Demon Slayer Corps

The Demon Slayer Corps (鬼殺隊) is the main protagonistic organization in the anime and manga series Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. They are an organization of swordsmen that existed since ancient times in Japan in order to protect humanity from the Demons.
